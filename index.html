About Us

OUR STORY

At Vel Spa, we blend time-honored techniques with a serene, modern environment to offer a holistic wellness experience. Our highly trained Hilot therapist are specialize in traditional Filipino techniques that aim to restore balance, relieve stress, and heal both body and spirit. Each session is personalized to address your specific needs with incorporating coconut oil, banana leaves, and hot stones to promote circulation, release tension, and detoxify the body.

More than just a spa, Vel Spa is a cultural experience. It is a celebration of Filipino heritage, hospitality, and wellness. Whether you're seeking relief from physical discomfort or simply want to reconnect with your inner peace, we invite you to relax, restore, and rediscover wellness the traditional way.

Come experience the warmth of Filipino hospitality and the healing power of Hilot only at Vel Spa.
