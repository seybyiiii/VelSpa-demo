About Us

OUR STORY

At Vel Spa, we blend time-honored techniques with a serene, modern environment to offer a holistic wellness experience. Our highly trained Hilot therapist are specialize in traditional Filipino techniques that aim to restore balance, relieve stress, and heal both body and spirit. Each session is personalized to address your specific needs with incorporating coconut oil, banana leaves, and hot stones to promote circulation, release tension, and detoxify the body.

More than just a spa, Vel Spa is a cultural experience. It is a celebration of Filipino heritage, hospitality, and wellness. Whether you're seeking relief from physical discomfort or simply want to reconnect with your inner peace, we invite you to relax, restore, and rediscover wellness the traditional way.

Come experience the warmth of Filipino hospitality and the healing power of Hilot only at Vel Spa.

How it all started

Vel Spa was established in February 2017 by a family of Spa enthusiasts in Marikina.

Inspired by their personal experience as avid customers here and abroad, Max Velasco’s family came up with an idea to create their own brand of a soothing massage service, capturing elements they wish were given more attention to by existing Spa businesses in the Philippines.

Particularly, they wanted this brand to highlight our very own traditional practices, in the form of Hilot. They wanted to make use of techniques relevant to Filipinos, further promoting our identity and pride, through proven and effective wellness solutions.

Vel Spa’s ultimate dream is for Pinoy Hilot to have a stronger presence than ever before, and be the best brand in the field, as it fills a gap in our local spa market currently dominated by Thai or other Asian and Western-influenced massage offerings.
